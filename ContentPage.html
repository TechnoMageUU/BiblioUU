Books

Pagans are like Unitarian Universalists, we've been told - they insulate their houses with books. We don't know if that's literally true, but both Pagans and UUs do enjoy a good read!

Many members and friends of CUUPS have requested reading lists. We knew where to get the best recommendations - from CUUPS' members and friends!
